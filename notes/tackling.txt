Tackling
--------
- Is tackling like taking possession?

- ie, The player just moves towards the other
  - The game then decides between competing players

- You can't tackle if
  - The other player is more than 5m away
  - You already have the ball
  - The other player does not have the ball
  - You are goalkeeper
    - Goalkeeper is in his circle
  - The other player is in your own team
